Collection

The "text on image background" design is really hard. Let's talk about something:

  • Colour issue: These beautiful painting are from public domain. But when consider compoments' colour set, here comes problem: If compoments' colour set too light, compoments will confused with background, but if too dark, compoments will break whole background.
  • Centre issue: If check page structure, you will found that articles are under background: yes, these background are actually divs. The whole articles actually need to use left and right properties so that block articles can "put" into background divs. However, left and right are difficult to put into centre. After hours and hours, I finally abandon put these articles into centre.
  • Beautiful, but even not suitable on mobile.
